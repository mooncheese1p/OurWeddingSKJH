♥ Notes that matter ♥

When to goearly–mid Dec

Christmas markets land on your Montreux, Lucerne & Zurich stops, and the mountains have reopened after November maintenance. Avoid Dec 18–Jan 2 — holiday peak nearly doubles flight prices. Mid-January is the cheapest alternative.

Rail: pass vs. half-fare

In 2026 the 6-day Swiss Travel Pass (CHF 399 pp) is no longer an automatic win. Run your exact legs through the official SBB calculator against two Half Fare Cards (CHF 150 pp) before buying.

Honeymoon touches

Tell each hotel it's your honeymoon when booking — free upgrades and small extras are common. Splurge the lake-view nights in Montreux and a Jungfrau-view room in Interlaken; keep Lisbon & Barcelona for memorable dinners since they're cheap.

Booking mechanics

Book the transatlantic as one multi-city ticket (cheaper than two one-ways). Book the European hops separately as one-ways, carry-on only. The Day 9 flex buffer protects a clear-sky Jungfraujoch run.
